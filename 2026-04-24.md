### [OpenAI Releases GPT-5.5, Bringing Company One Step Closer to an AI 'Super App'](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/23/openai-chatgpt-gpt-5-5-ai-model-superapp/)
**Source:** TechCrunch | **Published:** April 23, 2026
**Summary:** OpenAI launched GPT-5.5 on April 23, billing it as its "smartest and most intuitive" model yet. The model scores 88.7% on SWE-bench and 92.4% on MMLU with a 60% reduction in hallucinations versus GPT-5.4, supports a 1M-token context window, and is priced at $5/$30 per million input/output tokens via API. **Why it matters:** GPT-5.5 sets a new frontier benchmark in agentic coding and knowledge work, further compressing the cadence of flagship model releases across the industry.

### [EU Set to Classify ChatGPT Under Strict Online Platform Rules](https://www.computing.co.uk/news/2026/legislation-regulation/eu-to-classify-chatgpt-under-strict-online-platform-rules)
**Source:** Computing | **Published:** April 2026
**Summary:** The European Commission is preparing to designate ChatGPT as a "very large online search engine" under the Digital Services Act (DSA), with an announcement expected within days. ChatGPT surpassed 120 million monthly EU users in late 2025, clearing the 45-million threshold. Once designated, OpenAI would have four months to meet DSA requirements covering transparency, content moderation, and algorithmic accountability.
**Why it matters:** A DSA designation would place ChatGPT under the same regulatory tier as Google Search and Bing, marking the most significant AI-specific regulatory action in the EU since the AI Act.

### [Google Gemma 4: Byte for Byte, the Most Capable Open Models](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/technology/developers-tools/gemma-4/)
**Source:** Google Blog | **Published:** April 2, 2026
**Summary:** Google released Gemma 4 under the Apache 2.0 license in four model sizes (2B, 4B, 26B MoE, 31B Dense), all supporting multimodal inputs (text + image) with a 256K context window. The 31B Dense variant outperforms models 20× its size on several reasoning benchmarks, and edge variants run on devices as small as Raspberry Pi.
**Why it matters:** Gemma 4 sets a new performance-per-parameter record for open-weight models, putting frontier-grade multimodal capability within reach of any developer without API costs.

### [White House Unveils National AI Legislative Framework](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2026/03/president-donald-j-trump-unveils-national-ai-legislative-framework/)
**Source:** The White House | **Published:** March 20, 2026
**Summary:** The Trump administration released its National Policy Framework for Artificial Intelligence, a legislative guidance document urging Congress to act across seven areas: child protection, infrastructure impacts, intellectual property, free speech, innovation, workforce development, and preemption of state AI laws.
