### [New York's RAISE Act Signed Into Law, Takes Effect January 2027](https://www.governor.ny.gov/news/governor-hochul-signs-nation-leading-legislation-require-ai-frameworks-ai-frontier-models)
**Source:** Office of Governor Hochul (NY) | **Published:** March 27, 2026
**Summary:** New York Governor Hochul signed the Responsible AI Safety and Education (RAISE) Act, requiring developers of frontier AI models (trained with >10²⁶ FLOPs, compute cost >$100M, annual revenue >$500M) to publish safety frameworks, report incidents, and disclose risk assessments. The New York DFS will enforce the law with penalties up to $3 million for repeat violations, effective January 1, 2027.
**Why it matters:** New York's RAISE Act is the most substantive US state-level AI safety law yet enacted and is expected to become a national compliance template.

### [White House Releases National AI Policy Framework with Call to Preempt State Laws](https://www.consumerfinancemonitor.com/2026/04/08/the-white-houses-national-policy-framework-for-artificial-intelligence-what-it-means-and-what-comes-next/)
**Source:** White House / Consumer Finance Monitor | **Published:** March 20, 2026 (analysis April 8)
**Summary:** The White House released its National Policy Framework for AI, recommending Congress preempt state AI laws that "impose undue burdens," effectively proposing a single national standard over fifty competing state regimes. The Framework also recommends age-verification requirements for AI services accessed by minors and explicitly opposes creating any new federal AI regulatory body.
**Why it matters:** Federal preemption of state AI law would nullify statutes like California's and New York's RAISE Act, making this the most consequential AI governance battle of 2026.

### [AI Energy Efficiency Breakthrough Claims 100× Reduction via Neuro-Symbolic Hybrid](https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/04/260405003952.htm)

**Summary:** Researchers published findings showing that combining neural networks with human-like symbolic reasoning can reduce AI energy consumption by up to 100× while simultaneously improving accuracy on structured reasoning tasks.
