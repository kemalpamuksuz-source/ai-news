### [Anthropic Unveils Claude Mythos Preview in Restricted Cybersecurity Initiative](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/07/anthropic-mythos-ai-model-preview-security/)
**Source:** TechCrunch | **Published:** April 7–12, 2026
**Summary:** Anthropic released Claude Mythos Preview — its most capable frontier model to date — under a tightly controlled initiative called Project Glasswing, giving access exclusively to 12 partner organizations including Amazon, Apple, Microsoft, Cisco, CrowdStrike, and the Linux Foundation. The model autonomously discovered thousands of previously unknown zero-day vulnerabilities across major operating systems and browsers by reading code, forming hypotheses, and generating proof-of-concept exploits. **Why it matters:** Mythos Preview is the clearest signal yet that frontier AI has crossed into territory where offense/defense dual-use risk requires proactive containment rather than standard release pipelines.
