### [Trump Unveils National AI Legislative Framework, Seeking to Preempt State Laws](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2026/03/president-donald-j-trump-unveils-national-ai-legislative-framework/)
**Source:** The White House | **Published:** March 20, 2026
**Summary:** The Trump administration released its National Policy Framework for Artificial Intelligence, sending legislative recommendations to Congress that would establish a unified federal approach to AI regulation. The framework's most controversial plank would preempt state-level AI laws deemed to impose "undue burdens," while stopping short of creating any new federal AI regulator — instead routing oversight through existing sector-specific bodies and regulatory sandboxes. Other pillars cover child protections, deepfake abuse, data center energy permitting, IP rights, and AI workforce education.
**Why it matters:** If enacted, this would be the most sweeping U.S. federal AI law ever passed, reshaping the patchwork of state-level rules — and setting the stage for a major fight over federal vs. state authority over AI.
