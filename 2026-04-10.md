### [Anthropic Debuts Claude Mythos Preview in Restricted Cybersecurity Initiative "Project Glasswing"](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/07/anthropic-mythos-ai-model-preview-security/)
**Source:** TechCrunch | **Published:** April 7, 2026
**Summary:** Anthropic announced Claude Mythos Preview — its most powerful model to date, positioned above the existing Opus tier — and simultaneously revealed it will not be made publicly available. Instead, Anthropic is channeling access through Project Glasswing, a cybersecurity initiative giving over 50 partner organizations access with $100M+ in usage credits. ### [Meta Releases Llama 4 Maverick and Scout: Open-Weight Multimodal MoE Models](https://techcrunch.com/2025/04/05/meta-releases-llama-4-a-new-crop-of-flagship-ai-models/)
**Source:** TechCrunch | **Published:** April 5, 2026
**Summary:** Meta launched Llama 4 Scout and Llama 4 Maverick, the first open-weight models built on a mixture-of-experts (MoE) architecture with native multimodal capability (text, image, video) from the ground up. **Why it matters:** Llama 4's scale of deployment across Meta's billions of monthly active users gives open-weight models an unprecedented real-world testing ground that no other open model can match.

### [Neuro-Symbolic AI Slashes Robot Energy Use 100x While Boosting Accuracy](https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/04/260405003952.htm)
**Source:** ScienceDaily | **Published:** April 5, 2026
**Summary:** Researchers from Tufts University published results showing their neuro-symbolic vision-language-action (VLA) system achieves a 95% success rate on the Tower of Hanoi planning task — compared to 34% for standard neural approaches — while using only 1% of the training energy and 5% of the operational energy. Training time dropped from 36+ hours to 34 minutes. The work will be presented at the International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA) in Vienna in May 2026.
**Why it matters:** As AI's electricity consumption surpasses 10% of U.S. grid demand, a 100x efficiency gain in robotic reasoning systems could prove critical for scaling physical AI without unsustainable energy costs.
